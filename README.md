ReallyConnect is a mentorship platform that transforms cold networking into meaningful, structured connections through AI-powered request workflows.

Even within supportive communities like ColorStack, junior members often struggle to secure referrals and mentorship. Requests for guidance frequently go unanswered, not because of a lack of goodwill, but due to overwhelming message volume and unstructured communication.

ReallyConnect replaces broken networking patterns with a respectful, efficient system that protects mentor time while empowering mentees to make high-quality, intentional connection requests.

ğŸ¯ Problem Alignment

ReallyConnect directly addresses three major issues in modern professional networking:

1. Communication Void

Talented junior members send genuine mentorship and referral requests that often receive no response, leading to discouragement and isolation.

2. Request Fatigue

Alumni and industry professionals receive too many unstructured messages to reasonably respond to, even when candidates are strong.

3. Referral Lottery

Success depends on timing and visibility instead of a fair, structured system.

Our Solution

ReallyConnect introduces AI-powered, structured request workflows that:

Help mentees craft high-quality, intentional requests

Organize mentor inboxes

Respect professional time

Increase meaningful engagement
